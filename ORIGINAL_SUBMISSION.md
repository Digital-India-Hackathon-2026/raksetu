## Idea
Raksetu is a web app acting as a digital lifeline connecting donors, patients, and blood banks. It eliminates the panic of searching during emergencies. Patients can instantly broadcast location-based requests. Blood banks maintain live, visible inventories. For donors, it tracks eligibility and uses smart geo-alerts to notify them when their specific blood type is needed nearby. Supported by an AI chatbot, it seamlessly bridges the gap to save lives when every second counts. By transforming scattered data into real-time connections, the platform ensures that critical supply meets urgent demand without dangerous delays. Ultimately, Raksetu replaces the chaos of frantic social media appeals with a structured, instant, and highly reliable community network.
